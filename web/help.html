Account Setup

Registration

Your AgoraIQ account is created during Stripe checkout using your email. After subscribing, sign in at bot.agoraiq.net to access the web dashboard, or link your Telegram via /connect in the bot.

Authentication

The web app authenticates via a bearer token stored in your browser. This token is used for all API calls. If you get logged out, simply sign in again from the dashboard.

Telegram Linking

Telegram linking is one-time and secure. The bot generates a unique code that you claim through the web interface — no passwords are ever exchanged via Telegram. Use /disconnect to unlink at any time.

Tip: After linking, use /status in Telegram to verify your connection and see your current plan tier.

Signals

AgoraIQ generates and aggregates trading signals from multiple sources:

  • Scanner signals — Generated by the market scanner when breakout conditions are detected.
  • Provider signals — Scraped from verified Telegram and Discord signal providers, then parsed and normalized.
  • User signals — Submitted by you via /track or the Track page.

Every signal passes through the canonical signal schema. Each signal carries:

  • Symbol — Always in XXXUSDT format (e.g., BTCUSDT)
  • Direction — LONG or SHORT
  • Entry, Stop Loss, Targets — Up to 3 take-profit levels
  • Confidence — 0–100 AI score based on signal strength
  • Status — OPEN → TP1/TP2/TP3/SL/EXPIRED/CANCELLED
Plan gating: PRO users get real-time signals with full details. ELITE adds AI score breakdown, provider rankings, and advanced analytics.

Signal Tracking

Signal Tracking lets you submit your own trade ideas and have AgoraIQ monitor them against live market data. When price hits your targets or stop loss, the signal resolves automatically.

How to submit

From the Track page or via /track in Telegram, paste a signal in any common format:

BTCUSDT LONG Entry: 65000 TP1: 67000 TP2: 69000 SL: 63000

Signal lifecycle

  • OPEN — Actively tracked against TP/SL every cycle.
  • TP1 / TP2 / TP3 — Take-profit level hit. PnL recorded.
  • SL — Stop loss hit. Loss recorded.
  • EXPIRED — Aged out without hitting any level (default: 7 days).
  • CANCELLED — Manually cancelled.

Your stats

The Track page shows personal win rate, best/worst signals, and monthly breakdown. In Telegram: /my_stats.

Limits: PRO — signal tracking with core analytics. ELITE — full lifecycle with AI score, expectancy, drawdown analytics.

Market Scanner

The scanner monitors the crypto derivatives market every 5 minutes for:

  • Breakout candidates — Pairs approaching key levels with rising volume.
  • Volume spikes — Unusual activity relative to baseline.
  • Open interest shifts — Position building or liquidation signals.
  • Funding rate extremes — Crowded trade indicators.
  • Momentum scores — Multi-factor ranking across pairs.

Access via Scanner page or Telegram: /market, /top, /momentum, /volume, /oi, /funding.

Note: PRO and ELITE get real-time updates.

Provider IQ

AgoraIQ tracks signal providers from Telegram and Discord, scoring them by actual performance.

Each provider's score is derived from:

  • Win rate (TP vs SL)
  • Average return per signal
  • Consistency over time
  • Signal volume (more data = more reliable)

Follow providers with /follow ID. See the Provider IQ leaderboard.

The proof engine is public — no login required.
