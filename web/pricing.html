Questions

Can I cancel anytime?▾
Yes. Cancel from your Stripe customer portal — no lock-in, no questions. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
What's the difference between monthly and yearly?▾
Same features either way. Yearly plans are billed as a single annual charge at a meaningful discount versus 12 months at the monthly rate — see the current savings figure on the monthly/yearly toggle above.
Is there a free trial?▾
Trial details are loaded from our billing service — see the badge above the plan cards.
What payment methods are accepted?▾
All major credit/debit cards via Stripe. Payments are processed securely — AgoraIQ never stores your card details.
What does "signal lifecycle tracking" mean?▾
Submit any trading signal and AgoraIQ tracks it against live market data. Every state change — from open through TP hits, SL, or expiry — is recorded with full audit trail. Elite includes advanced analytics: win rate, expectancy, duration, and drawdown views.
Do I need to link my exchange?▾
No. AgoraIQ tracks signals using public market data from Binance and Kraken. No exchange API keys needed.
